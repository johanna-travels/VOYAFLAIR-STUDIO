About Me

Hi, I’m Joanna — the creator behind Voyaflair.

Based in Oslo and driven by a deep curiosity for the world, I created Voyaflair as a space to share the places that leave a lasting impression.

Through travel, I’m constantly drawn to destinations that offer a strong sense of atmosphere — from serene landscapes to hidden corners that reveal the true character of a place. For me, travel is not only about seeing new destinations, but about experiencing how a place feels.

Through photography, video, and storytelling, I aim to capture the mood, textures, and moments that make each destination unique. Voyaflair is where I document these experiences and transform them into visual stories designed to inspire more thoughtful and meaningful travel.
